Set Up in Parallel — Day 1

Free platforms where the actual work comes from. Create profiles now so you're discoverable while you learn.

    Phase 1 — Calibrate & Position (Days 1–30)

    CTOx pillar: the "pro mindset" + define your opportunity. Who do I serve, what do I sell, why me?

    Week 1–2: The mindset shift (employee → operator)

      Week 2–3: Understand the fractional model

        Week 3–4: Define your wedge

          Deliverable: Write your one-line positioning — "I help [who] do [what] so they [outcome]." Draft both an AI-specialist variant and a general-FCTO variant; pick one to lead with.

          Phase 2 — Package & Attract (Days 31–60)

          CTOx pillars: attract + the front half of convert. Turn expertise into an offer CEOs understand. Build credibility before you ever ask for a sale.

          Week 5–6: Design your offer and pricing

            Deliverable: Draft a 2–3 tier service menu (advisory / fractional / interim) with value-anchored pricing.

            Week 6–7: Build the credibility engine

              Deliverable: Publish 2 short LinkedIn posts or one teardown-style piece. Start building the public track record now while you still have the Microsoft title.

              Week 7–8: Activate the network

                Deliverable: List 30 warm contacts (IIT Bombay alumni, Microsoft AI alumni who've gone to startups, PNW VC contacts). Begin 5 reconnect conversations/week — no pitch, just reconnecting and listening.

                Phase 3 — Convert & Serve (Days 61–90)

                CTOx pillars: convert + serve. Turn conversations into signed retainers, then deliver in a way that renews.

                Week 9–10: Sales conversations and proposals

                  Deliverable: Write a reusable one-page proposal template and a simple MSA/SOW outline. Run your first 2–3 real discovery calls.

                  Week 10–11: Delivery frameworks (so the first engagement renews)

                    Deliverable: Build your "first 30 days of an engagement" playbook — the audit, the quick win, the roadmap.

                    Week 11–12: Show ROI and set up renewal

                      Deliverable: Build a simple one-page ROI/impact template you'll present at the end of every engagement to justify renewal and rate increases.

                      Bonus Track — Your AI Differentiator

                      Optional, in parallel. Free resources to sharpen the "AI Fractional CTO" wedge that sets you apart from generalists.

                        Deliverable: A short "AI build vs buy" decision framework you can run as a paid workshop — a natural foot-in-the-door offer.

                        The 90-Day Outcome

                        By Day 90 you should have: a written positioning and service menu, value-based pricing you can defend, a small body of published content, 30+ warm contacts re-engaged with 10+ real conversations, profiles live on Bolster and Go Fractional, a reusable proposal + delivery + ROI playbook, and ideally 1–2 discovery calls converting toward a first pilot. That's the same finish line CTOx sells — assembled for the cost of your time.
